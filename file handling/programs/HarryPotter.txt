The Harry Potter novel series, written by J.K. Rowling, consists of seven books chronicling the life of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, as they navigate magical education at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and battle the dark wizard Lord Voldemort.
    The series, published between 1997 and 2007, is a global phenomenon, translated into many languages and adapted into a major film franchise.
